About

I've been making games as a hobby since high school. I've always been the quiet kid in the back of the class, staring out of the window, lost in my imagination — and video games were where that imagination lived. I started with Game Maker, making small games for my friends, then fell deep into RPG Maker. Most of my early work was in RPG Maker 2003, but over the last two years I've been diving seriously into Unity and C#.

I've always been both an artist and a designer, but I got into coding too because I want to be able to build anything I can imagine. My academic background helped me pick up C# quickly, and having art and code under one roof means I can take a game from concept to a complete, playable experience entirely on my own.

Outside of games, I studied automation at University of West Attica and I'm currently working as an engineer on industrial applications — programming PLCs. Game development and art have been a hobby so far but I would consider game developer roles where I can contribute to a team, keep growing, and help make something people love to play.
